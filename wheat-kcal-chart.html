Kcal from wheat products per capita per day

How to read the graph

This graph plots the kcal from wheat per capita per day for different countries from the earliest year than data exists in the FAO database to 2011 as a single line. The data varies over time but using a straight line shows trends over time. Hover over lines to see country and data points. Hover over countries in the legend to highlight them in the graph. Click on the countries name to isolate that particular country. Click on the name again to show all countries. Click on the tick to show/hide certain countries. Scroll up and down the legend to see all the available countries.
